About App

Welcome to our website !

Discover simplicity in waste classification with our user-friendly app!. Powered by machine learning, it effortlessly distinguishes organic and inorganic waste, ensuring easy and effective recycling.

Join us on our mission to create a greener future, one user-friendly step at a time.

App Features

User-Friendly Interface

With intuitive navigation and clear instructions, users can easily capture images of waste items, and receive instant classification results

Image Recognition Technology

Users can simply capture an image of the waste, and the app will identify whether it is organic or inorganic, aiding in proper waste disposal.

Voice Assistance

Upon waste classification, the app provides a spoken response, informing users about the type of waste detected.
